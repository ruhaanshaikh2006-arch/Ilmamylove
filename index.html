Forgive Me, Ilma

Aap meri Princess... Aap meri Baby... Aap meri Angry Bird...

"Aapki khamoshi meri rooh ko cheer deti hai,
Meri har khushi ab aapki raahon mein bikhri hai.
Maante hain gunaah kiya hai humne bahut bada,
Par har saans ab aapki maafi ki muntazir khadi hai."

Ilma, main aaj yahan kisi safayi ke liye nahi, balki apne gunahon ka poora iqrar karne aaya hoon. Main jaanta hoon ki maine aapke bharose ko chaur-chaur kiya hai, aur us dil ko dukhaya hai jisne mujhe be-panah mohabbat di. Main sharminda hoon Ilma, main sach mein bahut sharminda hoon. Aapki aankhon mein jo aansu maine diye hain, wo mere wajood par ek bojh hain jo mujhe chain se jeene nahi dete. Maine aapke har khwab ko apni nadani se chheen liya, aur aapko wo saza di jo aap kabhi deserve nahi karti thin. PLEASE FORGIVE ME MY LOVE.

Mujhe pata hai ki 'sorry' keh dena kafi nahi hai. Maine aapko us waqt dukh diya jab aapko meri sabse zyada zaroorat thi. Maine apni ego aur apni galatiyon ko aage rakha aur aapki masoomiyat ko bhool gaya. Lekin Ilma, main qasam khata hoon Allah ki, maine khud ko badla hai. Main wo purana shakhs nahi raha jisne aapko rualaya. Maine apne andar ki har us burayi ko khatam kar diya hai jo humare darmiyan aayi thi. Main wo mard ban'na chahta hoon jo aapki hifazat kare, aur aapki zindagi ki har khushi ka sabab bane.

Aap shayad mujhse nafrat karti hon, aur aapka pura haq hai. Lekin meri nafrat sirf mujhse hai, aapke liye sirf be-inteha mohabbat aur izzat hai. YOU REMAIN IN EVERY SECOND OF MY LIFE. Main aage nahi badh sakta Ilma, kyunki aage badhne ka matlab hai aapke bagair jeena, aur wo mere liye bilkul namumkin hai. Meri har subah aapke khayal se shuru hoti hai aur har raat aapki yaadon mein khatam. Main adhoora hoon, main bikhra hua hoon, main bilkul akela hoon aapke bagair.

Ilma, please ek baar meri aankhon mein dekh kar mehsoos kijiye ki main kitna badal gaya hoon. Main aapke pairon ki dhool ban kar rehne ko taiyar hoon, bas mujhe ek aakhri mauka dijiye ki main aapke har zakhm ko bhar sakoon. PLEASE COME BACK TO ME ASAP. Humari dastan aisi khatam nahi honi chahiye thi. Main aapko wo sukoon dena chahta hoon jo maine aap se chheena tha. Aap meri duniya hain, aapke bagair main sirf ek zinda laash hoon.

Ilma, mera dil khoon ke aansu ro raha hai. Laut aaiye, hum sab kuch theek kar denge. Main hamesha sirf aapka banke rahunga.

"Mere gunaahon ne aapse aapki muskaan chheen li,
Meri nadaniyo ne hamari duniya hi chheen li.
Aapki har saza mujhe poori tarah manzoor hai,
Par aapke bina jeene ki himmat ab mujhme nahi."

Will you let me earn you back, Ilma?

Shukriya meri Princess! I will love you and protect you forever! ❤️
